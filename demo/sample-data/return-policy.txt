RETURN & REFUND POLICY
FurnishPro Office Furniture Store
Last Updated: February 2026

OVERVIEW
At FurnishPro, we want you to be completely satisfied with your office furniture purchase. We offer a straightforward return and refund policy to give you peace of mind when shopping with us.

RETURN WINDOW
You may return most items within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund or exchange. Items must be returned in their original condition and packaging. We do not accept returns after 30 days from the date of delivery.

ELIGIBLE ITEMS FOR RETURN
The following items are eligible for return:
- Office chairs (executive, ergonomic, task chairs) returned unused and unassembled
- Desks and workstations returned in original flat-pack packaging
- Filing cabinets and storage units returned in original packaging
- Accessories such as monitor arms, keyboard trays, and desk organizers

NON-RETURNABLE ITEMS
The following items cannot be returned:
- Custom or made-to-order furniture items (e.g. desks cut to specific dimensions)
- Items that have been assembled, used, or damaged after delivery
- Clearance or final sale items marked as non-returnable
- Items without original packaging

CONDITION REQUIREMENTS
All returned items must be:
- Unused and unassembled (for flat-pack furniture)
- In original factory packaging with all parts, hardware, and manuals included
- Free from damage, scratches, or modifications caused by the customer

HOW TO INITIATE A RETURN
To start a return, please contact our support team via the chat widget or email us at returns@furnishpro.co.ke within the 30-day return window. You will receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and instructions on how to ship the item back to us. Do not return items without an RMA number as they will not be accepted.

RETURN SHIPPING COSTS
- If the item is faulty, damaged on arrival, or we sent the wrong item, FurnishPro covers the full cost of return shipping.
- If you are returning an item because you changed your mind or ordered the wrong size, the return shipping cost is the customer's responsibility.
- For large furniture items such as executive desks or conference tables, we will arrange a collection. A collection fee of KSh 1,500 applies for change-of-mind returns within Nairobi. Outside Nairobi, collection fees vary by location.

REFUND PROCESSING
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund within 5 to 7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at the time of purchase. Bank transfers may take an additional 2 to 3 business days to reflect in your account depending on your bank.

EXCHANGE POLICY
If you would like to exchange an item for a different size, colour, or model, please initiate a return as described above and place a new order for the replacement item. We do not hold stock for exchanges, so placing a new order ensures you secure the item you want.

DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
If your item arrives damaged or with a manufacturing defect, please photograph the damage immediately and contact us within 48 hours of delivery via the chat widget. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund at no cost to you. Claims made after 48 hours of delivery may not be eligible for a free replacement.

WARRANTY
All FurnishPro furniture comes with a minimum 12-month manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Executive and ergonomic chairs carry a 24-month warranty. The warranty does not cover normal wear and tear, accidental damage, or damage caused by improper assembly.
